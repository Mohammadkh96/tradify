## Packages
recharts | For visualizing win rates and trade performance data
framer-motion | For smooth transitions, collapsible knowledge base sections, and entry animations
date-fns | For formatting dates in the trade journal
clsx | For conditional class names
tailwind-merge | For merging tailwind classes safely

## Notes
The app requires a "Dark Mode" default aesthetic to match the "Bloomberg Terminal" style.
Tailwind config should support 'font-mono' for financial data.
